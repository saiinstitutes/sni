Auxiliary Nursing & Midwifery

DETAILS OF THE PROGRAMME

The R-ANM programme is of 2 years duration (1.5 years of theory and practical’s and 6 months of internship), which includes clinical experience in district hospitals, primary and urban health centres, and sub-health centres under the government of Goa.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  • Age limit for admission: A candidate for admission to R-ANM Program, should complete 17years of age and should not exceed 35 years of age on or before 31st December 2021.
  • Educational Qualifications: Should pass 12thwith English as medium of instructions in Science, Arts, Commerce, Health Care Science recognized by CBSE/ ICSE / HSSCE or equivalent with 45% marks in aggregate.
  • Medically fit - should possess good physical and mental health to undergo this program.
  • Sex: Female
  • Knowledge of Konkani / Marathi is desirable.

RULES FOR ADMISSION:

  1. Candidates seeking admission for this program should fill and submit the application form attached to this Prospectus.
  2. Selection of the candidate will be done purely on merit.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:

Applications should be accompanied with Self Attested photocopies of each of the following documents.

  1. Certificate of Birth from the Registrar.
  2. Certificate of passing and statement of marks of 12th Std. examination.
  3. School Leaving Certificate and certificate stating Medium of instruction.
  4. Medical fitness certificate from Authorized Medical Practitioner.
  5. Certificate of Residence from the Mamlatdar of the concerned Taluka indicating period of continuous residence minimum last 10 years in Goa, proceeding the last date for the receipt of application.
  6. In case of the applicant belong to Schedule Caste /Schedule Tribe/Other Backward community of the State of Goa Certificate from Deputy Collector concerned to that effect.
  7. In case the applicant belongs to EWS of the state Goa, income and assets certificate from Mamlatdar of concerned taluka to that effect.
  8. In case of physically handicapped candidates a certificate from the constituted Medical Board of GMC Hospital Bambolim, Hospicio Hospital Margao & North Goa District (Asilo) Hospital Mapusa, regarding the nature and the extent to which the candidate is fit for pursuing the diploma (extend of disabilities should be 40% and above).

ATTENDANCE

  • The student must attend the classes in theory as well as practical. A minimum of 80% attendance in theory and in practical is required to be eligible to appear for final examination.
  • Any shortfall of practical attendance below 100%the student should make up in respective practical areas of absents before declaration of the result of final exams.

EXAMINATION

The institute will conduct two term examination in theory and practical for internal assessment during 1st and 2nd year, as per the regulations by the Indian Nursing Council Passing marks will be 50% in each subject in practical as well as theory. The students must pass in internal examination to be eligible for final examination conducted by Goa Nursing Council.

  1. At the end of 1st and 2nd year there will be final examination in theory and practical in each subject conducted by Goa Nursing Council. Each student has to pass in theory and practical exam separately in each paper.
  2. Candidates who fail in any subject can appear for a supplementary examination which will be held once in a year and within 6 weeks from the declaration of the results.
  3. Unless the student clears the first year examination she will not be able to appear in the second year final examination.

INTERNAL ASSESSMENT

Will be done by the score obtained in curricular activities listed below.

  1. Term examinations
  2. Unit test
  3. Assignments in each subjects.
  4. Practical posting
  5. Field visits reports
  6. Daily Diary
  7. Class room assessment
  8. Punctuality
  9. Practical experience record/Case book
