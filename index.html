GENERAL NURSING & MIDWIFERY (GNM)

General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM) is a three-year diploma program in the field of healthcare that trains individuals to become registered nurses and midwives. The curriculum focuses on providing knowledge and skills in patient care, medical procedures, and prenatal and postnatal care.

Know More


AUXILIARY NURSING & MIDWIFERY (ANM)

Auxiliary Nursing & Midwifery (ANM) is a healthcare profession that provides basic medical services and assistance in prenatal care and delivery. ANMs are trained to support registered nurses and midwives in delivering care to patients in a variety of settings. ANMs play an important role in improving access to quality healthcare in communities.

B.Sc
NURSING

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc Nursing) is a 4-year degree program that prepares students for a career as a registered nurse. The curriculum covers a wide range of subjects, including anatomy, physiology, pharmacology, and nursing practices. B.Sc Nursing graduates are equipped with the knowledge and skills to provide high-quality patient care in a variety of healthcare settings.
