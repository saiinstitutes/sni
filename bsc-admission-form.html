The Sai Nursing Institute, Sankhali, was founded in 2014 by The Sai Nursing Society, which was founded by Dr. Pramod Sawant. It is accredited by the Government of India Nursing Council in New Delhi and affiliated with the Goa Nursing Council in Bambolim, Goa. The institute is located  near Sankhali Community Health Center. The institute has an outstanding, hardworking, and versatile principal, as well as dedicated faculty. The institute has a reputation for academic excellence and the overall development of its students' personalities.
